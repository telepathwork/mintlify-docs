## Get started in three steps

Get your profile visible and apply for your first job.

### Step 1: Sign up for a free account

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Sign up" icon="copy">
    1. Click `Sign up`.
    2. Choose your sign up method.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Onboarding" icon="rectangle-terminal">
    1. Fill your first and last name.
    2. Complete a short survey.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 2: Set up your profile

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Edit your profile" icon="github">
    1. Go to `My profile`.
    2. Click `Edit`.
    3. Update your information.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Choose your username" icon="palette">
    1. Go to `Settings > Account`.
    2. Choose your username.
    3. Click `Save`.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Step 3: Apply for jobs

<Accordion title="Apply for a job" icon="rocket">
  1. Go to `Jobs` page.
  2. Search, filter, and click on a job you are interested in.
  3. Click `Apply`.
</Accordion>

Congratulations\! You've taken your first step to remote working future.
